Resonant Principles ☀️

This website contains curated principles, mental models, mindsets, and thoughts that resonate with me and have tremendously benefited my life.

A Principle is a general rules or guideline that is meant to be applied in a wide range of situations. Principles are often based on fundamental truths or laws, and they can be used to guide behavior, decision-making, and problem-solving. Principles are often abstract and can be difficult to define, but they are essential for understanding and predicting the behavior of complex systems.

A Mental Model is a cognitive structure that represents an individual's understanding of how something works. It can be thought of as a mental representation of a system or concept, and it helps us make predictions and decisions based on our understanding of that system. Mental models can be specific to a particular domain or subject, or they can be more general and apply to a wider range of situations.
